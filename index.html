What is PAIR tutor?

PAIR tutor is free to sign-up and allows you to quickly connect with other students on your campus for peer-to-peer tutoring. Whether you need consistent tutoring or you're stuck on a homework question due in a few hours, PAIR tutor will find you the smartest student for the job.

Tutors are the peers around you and anyone can teach as long as you are good at your subject! There is no application to be a tutor. You can charge your services hourly or a flat rate based on your own price.

How it works

1. Create a profile online or download the app and enter yourself as a tutor, mentee, or both!

2. If you claimed yourself a tutor, enter the classes and subjects you want to teach as well as your price.

3. Find a tutor by searching a specefic class (ex. Acc 2122) or by topic (ex. accounting 1).

4. After you have found a tutor, message them for a meetup! You pay them via PAIR tutor.

Why do we need PAIR tutor?

We believe in a community where people have a quick and easy way to connect and share peer-to-peer knowledge and talents.

You shouldn't be limited to professional tutoring prices, schedules, and the hassle of finding a tutor for a certain subject in your area.

And of course if you're good at a subject, why not get paid for it?
